Foolish Modesty

don't let any foolish modesty arrest your hands,
let it answer the call to its passions...

follow your instincts solemnly down its primal route,
and reign your virility over the fields of my flesh-full youth

debase my head if i try to protest,
lower me down into obedience of your sovereign requests

let me be yours - at praise and punishment,
watch closely and see that my desires stay fervent
at your swinging stroke or strike,
each is loved by me alike.

I take this all with pride and gratitude;
kneeling before you, with dewy eyes and seamless streams of endless tears and leaking cream,
yolky drops from my womanly wells,
where attraction brews and flows like hell,
blowing gales of wind that man sows in me,
by his forceful entrances and swift flees.

if i was not clear,
you may stress my desires until climactic rapture,
as embers fly,
till mayflies die,
and when Poseidon gets sick of the sea...

i will need only you, only ever you,
to make me bleed like late autumn sycamore trees

please conquer me west, east and south,
stopping only at the opening of my cavernous tomb,
(the place where man begets life marked by eros, ardor, and doom),
to seal me tight with love and a fate intertwined forever with you.
