✵ What's new, Rass? November 4th, 2025
forgive my dim mood - i'm not a fan of November. much rather i'd retell some of the memories of my trip up north into the wilderness, where me and a young woodwose enjoyed our tryst. one such memory occurred at 11:00 PM at night, where our natureman suggested that we walk out to the dock to stargaze. i agreed. so there we layed down supine, cold and utterly frozen spined, listless and shivering too, but looking upwards with eyes anew, for the cosmos had proudly bared her many auroral feathers. my companion, under a sort of compulsion spell, asked me every 145 seconds or so whether i was cold. i negated his claim. he pointed out that my shivers had shivers (which was true). but i wittingly turned our attention back upwards, because if i were to crystalize, i'd want my last impression to be that of wondrous gaze, not an envious side-glance at my much warmer, properly coated friend. alas, our cabinroom had a heated bed. so when it was time to return to the quarters, i was snug as a bug before the boy even stood up to his feet.
