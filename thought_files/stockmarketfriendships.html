Here I go, another dismal tirade that reflects nothing sweet, nothing daisy, nothing but the rotting tar that sits in the pit of my patience.

I rarely slip into such a lowly disappointment towards mankind. But again, it comes when you have seen some shit, bewildering and audacious behaviour to be exact, of those around you who you truly believed had better sense. I think the cynical truth I'm learning to accept is that selfishness is an aspect of people in general, and the act of lovebombing is a strategy done to preemptively apologize for the abandonment that's to come. When i was a child, i remember being taught not to lie. I honestly, seriously, fucking thought that i was making an oath that everyone was on board with. But no. Unfortunately for me, I missed the unanimous memo that life sends out at some point or another. I didn't know, for a very long time, that people were genuinely faltering; bending their words on a whim, crossing their fingers behind their back, smiling in the face of people they admire just to become the antithesis of it all. I figured it out very late, and I see the idiocy I was participant of. Did I really think that people, even those who i esteem to be much brighter, loving, and kinder than everyone else, would not have baseness too? That's what happens when your anthropological perspectives are fabricated from idolism and reverence.

But beneath the veil is something I can only describe as really hideous. Referring back to the original promise, that sinful fucking ode i chanted back to myself for years thinking it was a common prayer of sorts, is clear as crimson to me now. People are cheats, they are liars, they are fake as fuck, they are preachers of their own hushed urges, denouncers of their darkest secrets and sins, laying claim to peace as they carry on with vexed indifference, ignoring wars simply beyond their walls. They know economic love only, they understand friendship by the market fluctuations of their needs. As a result, my personal experience has been that the care, love and affection you show these creatures is only valued at the highest price only when they are at their lowest point. I take this too personally, because I thought, silly old me, I thought that you ought to do to others as you'd want done unto you!

But could it be? Is friendship really only a commodity people fight over when their heart fears its scarcity?

But yes, that's got to be what it is. Clearly, the midnight devilish sermon they attended told them that when your cup is full, and when your home chimes with laughter and warmth all around you, when the world has finally rewarded you with enough distractions, is when you must, emphasis on MUST, ignore the entire existence of your mopey melancholic friends, putting them indefinitely on stand-by.

I was an idiot, a fool, a miscreant to think that when I was busy, palm trees plotted around my every corner, sunshine blushing my cheeks all morning, endless calls without more than a dozen minutes by my lonesome, was when I would remember you. I was an idiot, a fool, a fucking naive girl to think that when you were depressed and lonely and hopeless, that you truly loved me more than life itself. But when you people move on, when you get engaged with life and marry the bliss around you, my importance fades in your mind. Don't deny this, and if you have the courtesy and cowardice to deny it to me, at the minimum don't deny it to yourself. Look closely, microscopically so, and behold the DNA pointing to a millennia of human hypocrisy, across one sinful species separated by bodies but united by self-centered conceit.

Bullshit.
