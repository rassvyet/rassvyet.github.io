Wraith

sometime in 2023

My welting weary heart,
left bloodless, wan, and pale,
was pacing lifelessly,
before an aimless looming trail.

This stray wraith,
although fateless and obtrusively inchoate,
never found herself moved by lures of gaity,
or tales of galliant resplenent strength.

Until one day,
a relic was found,
burrowed deep under her path,
this ravishing treasure had stayed,
embossed with dirt and sand,
waiting to be saved

before the wraith glanced down,
her ravenous senses were awoke,
needing not light or sound,
to realize her voracious heart was pulsing with stroke 

and so this treasure and wraith rejoiced,
not knowing the destitution they endured,
for living without the other was a curse,
but remaining together was a love so pure.
