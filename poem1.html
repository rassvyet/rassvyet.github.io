Fire, Songs & Valour

02/08/2025

With my love,
I could have filled ten centuries with fire, songs and valour; ten whole centuries, long and winding epochs of poetry and prose, penned to your name...
Many hymns for your amber eyes and their mosaic array; that glisten while you ruminate in somber tones, streaming tears that befall my lap - crying arrow's shot from Olympus' peak.
and that beauty... sonnets will dance to it forever; to your ivory skin, auburn hair with woodsy streaks of brown, a look so moving yet statuesque, you are left to wonder: who undid medusa's curse? Tell me, how many more years can i devote to those parting lips of yours? that speak to conceal the truth,
unmooring my trust in you.
Lastly, my beloved.../ confess: eternity would not even suffice to elucidate that beauty in your pain,
that kindness that grew out of the shadows,
that taught you the value of tenderness,
suce you were not handled so gently in your youth.
