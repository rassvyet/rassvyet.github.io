✵ Hear Ye, Hear Ye - October News!
.
It is the headmaster's birthday month!Hurrah! She has lots planned this month, we're told. There is a dignified Swede coming from abroad to perform on the Eve of her Majesty's birthday. Then, a chauffer will bring her home to a very tall elongated castle. In the following morning, we are told that another tall gentleman (beloved by her...Shh! tell no-one) will bring sushi to a graveyard for her enjoyment. Then, later in the month, towards the end, she will be embarking on a voyage to darker skies, where the stars bare their shiny gems, inches away from one's very eyes (or that is how it feels). Then, she will go into the cave, hopefully, we are told, to return.
