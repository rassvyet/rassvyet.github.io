The Daily Quid


Reports of scowling and howling have been made by several maids in the High court’s garden on late Thursday eve. They claim, as they clutched their nacre beads, that an absolutely unworldly beastial clamour was brewing up “yet again” in the decrepit tower above the barn house. Locals all know who dwells there.

“Oh it’s surely Quid getting up to something once again; only god knows what - I think he’s dragged that poor fawny donkey of his up those winding long steps. Poor thing. I feel awful”
said Lady Mann, a woman (if we can call her that) in her 30s, with a voice that itself could provoke the same complaints she casts… but anyways her friend chimed in, Lady Boye, a girl of ripe 28, (still unwed, if any bachelors are interested) curvaceous and supple in the right places, provided great insight in to the matter. In her words, the commotion was “lawt” [I think she is trying to say “loud”].

There were no comments, as is natural, from the high court’s guards themselves, though we can imagine that Muze Cles wanted nothing more than to protest that the jesters be moved even further away from the court. Instead, he spent that very morning of the disturbance riding his stallion off into the west banks. Maybe the guard takes this time for leisure because he knows that Quid’s noises would deter any thieves or bandits (should we ever find them within our Haven, blessed be the King! Long live King Dmitri).

Our reporters decided to pay a visit to the barn house and knock on its dampened wood board functioning as a door.
A voice was heard, two voices, in face, allegedly having the following conversation:

Person 1: Oh you turnip brain, you’ve brought the Gestapo to our door with your noise!

Person 2: Quid, we don’t gotta Gestapo. It’s probably the door sniffing elves. Don’t open the door!

Person 1: Qort, do you think they can hear us right now? Do they understand our language?

Person 2: not at all Quid. Why don’t you test it - tell them that the door is unlocked and that they can come right in! I bet they won’t know what you’re saying. But wait, let me first excuse myself and then say it.

Person 1: Ok….. ok you gone, Qort?….. HEY COME ON IN IF YOU DARE, THE DOOR’s UNLOCKED AND WE’RE READY FOR A GREAT SNIFFING

we took it as a legal approval to enter, so we did. The sight was nothing much out of the ordinary, just Quid Ruckinus waxing the donkey’s behind. We questioned the motive, to which he said that he needed to wax its hairs. We asked if he wasn’t sure he meant to apply wax, as in hair wax, onto the coat, to which he was so taken aback that all we heard was a fart, (emitting from him, not the barn animal in fact) that echoed in the tower for several seconds. Qort was heard chuckling at the top of the stairs.
